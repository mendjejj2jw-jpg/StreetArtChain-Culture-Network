# StreetArtChain Culture Network

## Overview

StreetArtChain Culture Network is a decentralized platform built on the Stacks blockchain that preserves and documents urban street art culture while protecting legitimate artists and preventing vandalism. The platform bridges the gap between street art documentation and community-driven preservation efforts.

## Vision

To create a comprehensive, decentralized registry that celebrates street art culture while distinguishing between legitimate artistic expression and unauthorized vandalism. Our platform empowers communities to document, verify, and protect urban art while rewarding contributors who help preserve cultural heritage.

## Core Features

### 🎨 Street Art Documentation Registry
- **Location-based Documentation**: Precise GPS coordinates and detailed location information
- **Artist Attribution**: Proper credit and verification for street artists
- **Cultural Significance Records**: Historical context and community impact documentation
- **Immutable Timestamps**: Blockchain-based proof of when art was documented
- **Community Validation**: Crowd-sourced verification of authenticity

### ✅ Artist Verification System
- **Identity Verification**: Secure process for artists to claim their work
- **Commission Status Tracking**: Differentiate between commissioned and unauthorized work
- **Portfolio Integration**: Link artists to their documented pieces
- **Reputation Building**: Track verified artists and their contributions
- **Mural Classification**: Categorize works by type, style, and legal status

### 🛡️ Vandalism Prevention Network
- **Community Alerts**: Real-time notifications about threats to documented art
- **Collaborative Protection**: Organize community efforts to preserve artwork
- **Damage Reporting**: Track and document vandalism attempts or deterioration
- **Restoration Coordination**: Connect artists with restoration opportunities
- **Legal Status Tracking**: Monitor permits and legal protections

### 🏆 Urban Culture Rewards
- **Documentation Incentives**: Token rewards for high-quality street art documentation
- **Artist Support**: Direct compensation for verified artists
- **Community Contributions**: Reward users who report vandalism or contribute to preservation
- **Cultural Impact Metrics**: Measure and reward positive community impact
- **Staking Mechanisms**: Long-term engagement and platform governance

## Technical Architecture

### Smart Contracts

#### 1. Street Art Documentation Registry
- **Function**: Central registry for all documented street art pieces
- **Key Features**: Location mapping, metadata storage, ownership tracking
- **Data Storage**: IPFS integration for image storage, on-chain metadata

#### 2. Artist Verification System  
- **Function**: Manages artist identities and work attribution
- **Key Features**: Identity verification, portfolio management, reputation scores
- **Security**: Multi-sig verification process, community validation

## Getting Started

### For Artists
1. Create an account and complete identity verification
2. Submit documentation of your street art pieces
3. Provide location details and artistic context
4. Earn rewards as your work gets verified and appreciated

### For Documenters
1. Install the mobile app or access the web platform
2. Discover undocumented street art in your area
3. Photograph and provide detailed location/context information
4. Earn rewards for quality contributions

### For Community Members
1. Browse documented street art in your neighborhood
2. Report vandalism threats or damage to documented pieces
3. Participate in community preservation efforts
4. Support artists through the platform's reward mechanisms
